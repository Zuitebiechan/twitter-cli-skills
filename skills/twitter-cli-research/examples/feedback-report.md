# Product feedback report template

## Scope
- Product / brand: example product
- Queries reviewed:
  - "example product"
  - "example product bug"
  - "example product feature"
- Result size: 20 items per query

## Positive signals
- Users appreciated speed, convenience, or core value delivery.
- Some posts highlighted specific use cases rather than generic praise.

## Negative signals
- Complaints clustered around reliability, missing features, or confusing setup.
- A few posts suggested friction in onboarding or expectations mismatch.

## Recurring requests
- Better documentation
- Improved reliability
- More explicit feature controls

## Confidence notes
- This is a lightweight scan, not a statistically representative survey.
- Repeated complaints across multiple queries are more meaningful than isolated comments.

## Recommended follow-up
- Track repeated complaint keywords over time
- Compare current results with future scans
- Separate high-signal practitioner feedback from low-context reactions
